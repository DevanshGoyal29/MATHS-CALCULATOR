FACTORIAL OF A NUMBER

Factorial Computation This tool calculates the factorial of a non-negative integer. The factorial function, denoted by the exclamation mark (!), represents the product of consecutive positive integers from 1 up to the given number. Factorials are fundamental in various mathematical and statistical computations, including permutations, combinations, probability theory, and series expansions. This calculator provides a rapid and accurate method for determining the factorial of any non-negative integer.
