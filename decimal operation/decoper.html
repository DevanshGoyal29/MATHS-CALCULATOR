OPERATIONS ON DECIMALS

Decimals represent numbers that are not whole numbers. They are used to express values between whole numbers, making them essential for precise measurements and calculations. Unlike whole numbers, decimals include a decimal point followed by digits that represent fractional parts. This tool allows you to perform various arithmetic operations on decimals, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Experience the versatility of decimals and their applications in everyday life through this calculator.
