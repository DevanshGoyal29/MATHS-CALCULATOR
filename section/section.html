SECTION FORMULA CALCULATOR

Section Formula Calculator - This online tool accurately calculates the coordinates of a point that divides a line segment in a given ratio. The section formula is a fundamental concept in coordinate geometry used to determine the position of a point between two known points. By inputting the coordinates of the endpoints of the line segment and the desired ratio, users can efficiently compute the coordinates of the dividing point. This tool finds applications in various fields, including physics, engineering, and computer graphics, where determining the position of points along a line is essential.
